About Us

TutorConnect is a collaborative effort by a group of college students dedicated to changing the way tutoring services are accessed in the Philippines. With a vision of enhancing educational support, we aim to provide a platform where learners and tutors can connect.

Our Team

Our team combines skills in web development, design, and user experience. Together, we bring a mix of technical expertise and understanding of the educational landscape in the Philippines. Our goal is to ensure that TutorConnect offers a practical solution for all your tutoring needs.

Our Story

The idea of TutorConnect was inspired by our adviser, who gave us a hint that sparked our imagination. We took that initial idea and expanded it, driven by our collective desire to make quality education accessible to everyone. As students, we understand the challenges of finding reliable and effective tutoring, and we set out to create a solution that addresses these needs.

What We Offer

At TutorConnect, we provide a directory of tutors across various subjects and levels. Our platform is not only focused on academic subjects but is also open to areas like dancing, singing, and more. As long as the tutor and learner can meet, TutorConnect is here to facilitate the connection. With features and an intuitive interface, TutorConnect makes the process of finding a tutor nearby simple.
